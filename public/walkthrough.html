Sales Orders

During an active visit, agents can book sales orders with product details, quantity, and pricing. The total is auto-calculated and the order details are emailed instantly.

  • Product/service name, description, quantity, and unit price
  • Auto-calculated total amount
  • Customer name and phone pre-filled from lead
  • Optional remarks for special instructions

Payment Collection

Collect payments in the field with support for multiple payment modes. Reference numbers are tracked for reconciliation.

  • Support for Cash, Cheque, UPI, Bank Transfer, Online
  • Payment reference number tracking
  • Instant email notification to configured address
  • History of all collections on the visit record

Payment Collection Mode

Switch to the "Collect Payment" tab to record payments received in the field. The payment mode determines which reference fields are shown.

  • Cash — Amount recorded, no reference needed
  • Cheque — Cheque number captured
  • UPI — UPI transaction reference
  • Bank Transfer — Transfer ID recorded
  • Online — Payment gateway reference

Agent: Submit Claims

Agents see their past claims and can submit new ones. The "Auto Calculate" button computes total distance from all GPS-verified visit check-in points for the selected date using the Haversine formula.

  • Select date and enter or auto-calculate distance
  • Amount auto-calculated: distance (km) × rate per km
  • Add route summary (e.g. "Office → Client A → Client B")
  • View all past claims with status badges
  • One claim per day (duplicate prevention)

Manager: Review & Recommend

Branch managers see all submitted claims from their direct reports. They can review each claim, add remarks, and recommend individually or use bulk recommend for efficiency.

  • See all team claims with agent name, distance, and amount
  • Select multiple claims with checkboxes
  • Bulk recommend with one click
  • Add optional remarks for HQ context

HQ Admin: Approve or Reject

Admins see all claims across the organization. Filter by status, select multiple claims, and perform bulk approve or bulk reject with optional rejection reasons.

  • Filter: Recommended, Submitted, Approved, Rejected, All
  • Summary cards: pending count and total approved amount
  • Bulk approve / bulk reject with reason
  • Full audit trail: who recommended, who approved, timestamps

Organization Settings

Configure Notifications & Rates

Admins configure organization-wide settings including the notification email for orders/collections and the flat rate per kilometer for travel reimbursements.

  • Notification Email — Where sales orders and payment collection reports are sent
  • Rate per KM — Flat rate used to calculate travel reimbursement amounts
  • Settings saved to organization profile, accessible from sidebar
  • Only visible to Admin, Super Admin, and Platform Admin roles
